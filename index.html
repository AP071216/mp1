Our Values

Respect

Here at Dog Dojo we believe that everyone involved should be treated with respect, this includes our furry friends. Having respect for our pets breeds a positive relationship between dog and owner

Patience

Patience is key when training your dog but also when educating others and ourselves. Forming a bond with an animal that can't speak our language can be tough, we aim to help bridge that gap.

Fun

Whilst owning a dog is an absolute pleasure, when we reach those tough times, fun tends to be pushed aside to handle behaviours. We aim to help you find the fun in training and owning a dog, even when times are tough.

Knowledge

We aim to help you understand your dog and their behaviours. Trainers, both experienced and undexperienced are always learning, we will always strive to improve our knowledege alongside you
