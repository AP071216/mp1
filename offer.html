What we offer

6 Week Course - Puppies 12-20 weeks old

Here at Dog dojo we offer a 6 week course for puppies aged between 12 and 20 weeks. We provide a safe and fun environment for your puppy to learn and socialise with other puppies. The course is designed to help your puppy learn basic commands, socialisation skills, and good manners. Our experienced trainers will guide you and your puppy through each session, providing you with the tools and knowledge you need to continue your puppy's training at home.

Curriculum

One of the most important exercises, we will help you achieve this even with distractions

Effective techniques for quick responses on voice cue every time

Positive way of teaching a puppy not to pull on a lead

Calm greetings between puppies allow for good manners and confidence

Teaching your puppy to settle in a safe place, this is a great skill for life or going out and about

Reducing the risk of puppy teeth to humans by learning how to play safely and redirecting nipping

Allowing your puppy to enjoy being brushed remaining calm during the process

Learning how to manage your puppy's fear of loud noises and improving responses to unfamiliar and "scary" objects

What we need from you

We require all our puppies to be fully vaccinated and have had a worming treatment to prevent cross infection between puppies. This is to ensure the safety of all puppies in the class. We also ask that you bring along a lead and collar for your puppy, as well as some treats to reward them for their good behaviour. We ask that you bring along a blanket or mat for your puppy to sit on during the class. This will help to create a comfortable and familiar space for your puppy. During week 2 we will be covering grooming so please bring a towel and a soft brush suitable for puppy coats.

1-2-1 Sessions

We offer 1-2-1 sessions for puppies and adult dogs. These sessions are tailored to your dog's individual needs and can be booked at a time that suits you. We can help with a range of issues, from basic obedience training to more complex behaviour problems. Our experienced trainers will work with you and your dog to develop a training plan that is right for you. We can also provide support and advice on a range of topics, including puppy socialisation, house training, and basic commands. Our 1-2-1 sessions are a great way to get the support and guidance you need to help your dog reach their full potential.

What we can help with:

  • Basic commands
  • Lead walking
  • Recall
  • House training
  • Socialisation
  • Behaviour problems
  • Fear of loud noises
  • Separation anxiety

Events

We offer a range of one off and regular events throughout the year, including workshops, seminars, and socialisation days. These events are a great way to meet other dog owners and learn more about dog training and behaviour. Our events are designed to be fun and informative, and are suitable for dogs of all ages and experience levels. We also offer a range of activities for you and your dog to enjoy together, including agility, scent work, and obedience training. We ask that your dogs are not aggressive and you are able to keep good control over them throughout the session to avoid risk to other people and their pets.

Advice

Here at Dog Dojo we want to ensure that everyone is able to receive advice about their pets, we are pleased to offer a free advice service that all our trainers are involved in. Just drop us a message on our contact page ensuring to select training advice and we will try and assist you as much as we can. Following our form submission we will be in touch via your email address to open communications to one of our trainers. In some cases we may suggest a 1-2-1 session however we will do our best to avoid this by providing multiple options for you to try at home. Whether you need advice on a training issue or care of your pet, we are here to provide non-judgemental and qualified advice.
