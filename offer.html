What we offer

6 Week Course - Puppies 12-20 weeks old

Here at Dog dojo we offer a 6 week course for puppies aged between 12 and 20 weeks. We provide a safe and fun environment for your puppy to learn and socialise with other puppies. The course is designed to help your puppy learn basic commands, socialisation skills, and good manners. Our experienced trainers will guide you and your puppy through each session, providing you with the tools and knowledge you need to continue your puppy's training at home.

Curriculum

One of the most important exercises, we will help you achieve this even with distractions

Effective techniques for quick responses on voice cue every time

Positive way of teaching a puppy not to pull on a lead

Calm greetings between puppies allow for good manners and confidence

Teaching your puppy to settle in a safe place, this is a great skill for life or going out and about

Reducing the risk of puppy teeth to humans by learning how to play safely and redirecting nipping

Allowing your puppy to enjoy being brushed remaining calm during the process

Learning how to manage your puppy's fear of loud noises and improving responses to unfamiliar and "scary" objects

What we need from you

We require all our puppies to be fully vaccinated and have had a worming treatment to prevent cross infection between puppies. This is to ensure the safety of all puppies in the class. We also ask that you bring along a lead and collar for your puppy, as well as some treats to reward them for their good behaviour. We recommend that you bring a toy for your puppy to play with during the class, as this will help to keep them engaged and focused. Finally, we ask that you bring along a blanket or mat for your puppy to sit on during the class. This will help to create a comfortable and familiar space for your puppy.
