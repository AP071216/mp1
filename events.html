Events

At Dog Dojo, we host a variety of events throughout the year to promote dog training, socialization, and fun! Our events are designed for dogs of all ages and skill levels. Whether you're looking to improve your dog's obedience, socialize with other dogs, or just have a great time, we have something for everyone.

Agility group

Group suitable for all levels, join us in a fun, informative and safe evnvironment to build new skills for you and your dog.

Midday walkers

Relaxed group for owners wanting a break midway through the day to spend time with their pet and socialise with others.

Obedience group

Informal obedience class for yourself and your dog, join us to learn how to take your dog's training to the next level.

Coffee and Walkers

On weekends we offer a group similar to midday walkers but with the added bonus of coffee and cake from local cafes and trailers.
